# Account Deletion Request - Nutrify

We value your privacy. If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from the Nutrify app, please follow the instructions below.

## How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to our support team:

*   **Email:** ibnuhabib017@gmail.com
*   **Subject:** Account Deletion Request - [Your Registered Email]

### Please include the following information:
1.  The email address associated with your Nutrify account.
2.  A brief statement confirming that you want to delete your account and all stored nutrition logs.

## What Happens Next?
Once we receive your request:
1.  We will verify your identity to ensure the security of your data.
2.  Within **7 business days**, we will permanently delete your account from our **Supabase** database.
3.  All your personal information, including meal logs and profile data, will be removed and cannot be recovered.

## Note on App Access
Deleting your account is permanent. If you wish to use Nutrify again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
