We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

### Types of Data Collected:
*   **Personal Data**: While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data").

## 2. Use of Data
We use the collected data to:

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use **Supabase** (a secure cloud database) to store your information. Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party marketers.

## 4. Google Sign-In
If you choose to use Google Sign-In, we collect your email and name from your Google account profile solely for authentication purposes within our App.
